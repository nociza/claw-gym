PROJECT ATLAS — Status Report
Prepared by: Lisa Nakamura, Finance Director
Date: December 20, 2024

Executive Summary
=================
Project Atlas remains a strategic priority. From a financial perspective, the project
requires a budget adjustment to account for infrastructure costs that were not in
the original estimate. I support the Q1 2025 launch timeline.

Budget Overview
===============
The current approved budget for Project Atlas is $750,000, reflecting the $250,000
supplemental allocation approved by the steering committee on November 1, 2024.
Total expenditure to date is $410,000. The supplemental funds were necessary to
cover cloud infrastructure scaling costs and additional security tooling.

- Core API development: COMPLETE (November 2024)

- Frontend integration: IN PROGRESS (estimated completion: January 2025)
- Production launch: SCHEDULED (Q1 2025)

Team Composition
================
Twelve team members are assigned to Project Atlas. Two additional contractors
were brought on in November for infrastructure work (covered by supplemental budget).

Risks

2. Supplemental budget has already been partially consumed — limited contingency remaining
3. Must finalize vendor contracts before end of Q1
